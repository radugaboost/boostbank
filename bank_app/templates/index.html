About us

Welcome to our bank! We offer tariffs, loans, accounts, and investment options in both stocks and cryptocurrencies to help you achieve your financial goals. Our focus is on providing affordable and secure banking services to our customers. Thank you for choosing our bank to help you manage your money and grow your wealth.

Tariffs

Current Tariff

  • Monthly account fee of $10
  • Unlimited transactions
  • Free online and mobile banking
  • Free debit card
  • Free first checkbook
  • Overdraft protection available

Savings Tariff

  • No monthly account fee
  • Interest rate of 1.5%
  • Free online and mobile banking
  • Free electronic transfers
  • Limited to six transactions per month
  • Minimum balance of $1,000

Deposit Tariff

  • Interest rate of 2%
  • Flexible deposit terms ranging from 3 to 60 months
  • Minimum deposit of $5,000
  • Penalty for early withdrawal
  • Free online and mobile banking
  • FDIC insured up to $250,000 per depositor, per insured bank

Credits

Student Loan

Invest in your education with our student loans. We offer competitive rates and flexible repayment terms to help you pay for tuition, books, and other education-related expenses. With our easy online application process, you can get approved quickly and start focusing on your studies.

Auto Loan

Get behind the wheel of your dream car with our auto loans. We offer financing for new and used vehicles, with flexible repayment terms and competitive interest rates. Our loan specialists will work with you to find the right loan for your budget and needs, so you can hit the road with confidence.

Business Loan

Grow your business with our business loans. We offer financing for a variety of business needs, including working capital, equipment purchases, and expansion projects. Our experienced loan officers will work with you to find the right loan for your business goals, so you can focus on growing your company.

Home Loan

Make your dream home a reality with our home loans. We offer a variety of loan programs, including fixed-rate and adjustable-rate mortgages, as well as refinancing options. Our mortgage experts will guide you through the application process and help you find the right loan for your budget and lifestyle.

Personal Loan

Get the funds you need for unexpected expenses, home repairs, or other personal needs with our personal loans. We offer flexible repayment terms and competitive interest rates to help you get back on track. Our loan officers will work with you to find the right loan for your budget and needs.

Investments

Stock
